We Provide Best Quality.

ArchiHomes is a New Delhi based firm that provides custom architecture and interior design services for varied projects including commercial, retail, hospitality and residential projects. Founded in 2001 by Principal Designer Mukeshbhai Tank, ArchiHomes offers a comprehensive range of interior design services and a broad collection of in house custom-designed furniture and lighting products. Committed to architectural integrity and accomplished in a broad scope of design styles. They have delivered innovative design solutions for a wide range of developments through their multidisciplinary expertise in the field of architecture and interiors.
